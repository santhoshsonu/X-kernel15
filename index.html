About X-Kernel

X-kernel is one of the most celebrated annual tech fest's at Gokaraju Rangaraju Institute of Engineering and Technology , which is being organised by the department of Computer Science, this year. It consists of a pool of technical events and contests that enthrall the code lovers, tests their mettle as a programmer and enhances the skills that are required to be a code master.
The event also contains a healthy mix of coding and fun that makes it more enthusiastic for the participants.
The technical events are categorised into --

  • Technical Events
  • Paper Presentations
  • Project Expo

H'Ja C C++

Organizer :
Ch Ujwal Vikas

Volunteers :
Any of X-kernel Team

Languages Used :
C, C++, Java, HTML

In this event there will be a bowl filled with different coloured paper rolls.Each colour depicts a language(eg: pink:C, blue:Java, yellow:HTML, red:C++).The participant can choose any color of their choice where a number will be present in that paper roll.

A question will be asked based on the number, if they answer the question correctly then a chance of gambling a dice is given to them.So, based on the number which they get from the dice will be moved forward.Minimum of two colors should be choosen while in the game.
Winner is choosen on the basis of the participant on the higher number.

All together, Its H'Ja C C++

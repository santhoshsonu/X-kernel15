Champion of the Champions.

X-Kernel trophy is given to the CHAMPION OF THE CHAMPIONS. A surprise round is conducted to the winners of all the X-Kernel events. The one who wins in this surprise round is the one who gets this trophy and will honoured with the title CHAMPION OF THE X-KERNEL. Also the champion's photo will feature on the X-Kernel's website.
